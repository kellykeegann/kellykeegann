Vest No. 3 is a chunky slipover, which fits perfectly on top of shirts and dresses.
The fit is short and wide with deep armhole openings.
The i-cord edges and the significant neckline add a feminine touch, while the rib at the bottom is made in length to compliment the body the best way possible.
The style is knit top down.

Sizes

XS (S) M (L) XL

Measurements

Length (center back): 50 (52) 54 (57) 59 cm

Width (circumference): 99 (104) 110 (116) 121 cm

Size guide:
Vest No. 3 is a short slipover with a rather wide fit.
The vest is worn with approx. 15-20 cm positive ease, measured around your bust.
If the vest is designed to be worn on top of a dress or combined with a shirt, you can advantageously take your measurements when wearing clothes.

RECOMMENDED NEEDLES

Circular needle 9 mm (60, 80 or 100 cm)

Circular needle 7 mm (60, 80 or 100 cm)

GAUGE

10.5 sts x 15 rows in stockinette stitch on needle 9 mm = 10 x 10 cm

Material

The vest is knit with 4 threads. 1 thread of each of the following

150 (150) 150 (200) 200 g or Double Soft Merino, Knitting for Olive 50 g / 110 m. Shown in 'Powder'.

100 (100) 100 (100) 100 g or Merino, Knitting for Olive 50 g / 250 m. Shown in 'Hokkaido'.

100 (100) 100 (100) 100 g or Merino, Knitting for Olive 50 g / 250 m. Shown in 'Caramel'.

50 (50) 50 (50) 50 g or Soft Silk Mohair, Knitting for Olive 25 g / 225 m. Shown in 'Sky Blue'.
