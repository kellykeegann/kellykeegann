About

Yarn & Wool

YARN & WOOL started with a crush on a knitted baby with stripes when I was expecting my first child back in 2015.

Back then I couldn't knit at all. But I decided that I had to learn that.

Then came a lot of baby clothes and children clothes. And in time, the desire to knit to myself arose.

In the absence of recipes, of exactly the things I dreamed of, I started developing my own recipes and YARN & WOOL was born.

My name is Louise, I am 32 years old and I live in Frederiskberg with my husband and our two daughters.

Haven't you guessed it already, then the name comes from one of my absolute favorite movies that has followed me since my childhood; Sound of Music.

Good knitwear!

Louise

Our Manifesto

Our mission is to inspire the next generation of makers through reinvention of the craft experience.

All for one and one for wool

We are constantly evolving to make sure we create and support craftly spaces where everyone feels welcome. That means making sure our patterns fit every size and shape, our customers see themselves reflects in our photography and our products are accessible for a range of abilities.

It's all about you

You've probably cottones on by now, but you, the gang, are the heart of everything we do - as they say, friends that make together, stay together. We have a global knitwork of gangstas, with a world of crafty knowledge at their needle tips. Join us to meet the crew, heas to pur Youtube for all the tutorials you could wish! We're only ever a click away.

Knit your bit

Once we've got you stitching, we want to use our collectives skills to effect positive change for the planet. Crafters are already doing their bit, providing a more sustainable production process by making things themselves. We saw an opportunity to be a catalyst for change, providing our makers with sustainably sourced material and bringing newbies into crafting by spreading the wooly gospel far and wide.

We never want to pull the wool over your eyes so if you want to know more about where one of our products originals from, or how its made, please get in touch or have a look at our sustainably page here.
