Beanie No. 2 is a classic rib knit hat.
The hat is knit in rounds on the round and ends with the magic loop method at the dec.
It is laid out with Italian layouts, for a nice finish on the hat's ribbed edge.

Sizes

XS (S) M (L)

HODEOMKRETS

Dimensions are set such that size is determined by your head circumference.
So you measure yourself, not the hat.
49-51 (51-54) 55-57 (58-61) cm

INDICATIVE PINS

Circular needle 3.5 mm (60 cm)
Possibly. stocking stick 3.5 mm

Knitting strength in rib on needle 3.5 mm: 31 m / 28 p = 10 x 10 cm

Remember that the stick size is indicative. Therefore, always make a knitting test before knitting and switching to another needle, if you do not achieve the right knitting strength.

Materials

The hats shown are knitted respectively in Alpakka from Sandnes (colored gray flour 1042) and in a combination of Arwetta from Filcolana (color 196 vanilla) together with a thread Knitting for Olive Silk Mohair col. Cloud.

100 (100) 100 (100) gr. Alpaca from Sandnes
100 m. / 50 gr or 50 (50) 100 (100) gr Pernilla from Filcolana 175 m / 50 gr.

Mohair edition:
25 (25) 25 (25) gr Knitting for Olive Soft Silk Mohair
50 (50) 50 (50) gr Arwetta from Filcolana 250 m / 50 gr.

The recipe is sent as a PDF file to your mail.
